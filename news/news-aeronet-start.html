AeroNet Officially Launched!

We are thrilled to announce the kick-off of AeroNet project, a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA) Staff Exchange, that will redefine in-flight connectivity and networking for the Beyond 5G and 6G era. AeroNet will train a new generation of researchers to tackle the grand challenge of seamless, ultra-reliable, and secure airborne communications, enabling the aircraft of the future to become intelligent network nodes — connected to terrestrial, non-terrestrial (satellite), and edge infrastructures
